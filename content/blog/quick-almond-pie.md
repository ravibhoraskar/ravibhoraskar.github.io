<p>With a lot (and I mean <strong>a lot</strong>) of time on my hands, I am dabbling my hands in a bit of everything, including cooking. As I was sitting on the dinner table today, I suddenly felt enlightened, much like Buddha did under the Bodhi tree. The heavens proclaimed that I head to the kitchen, and create a masterpiece that the Gods wish me to create. Now, who am I to dispute the Gods? The item that I created in my creative frenzy turned out to be....well not too bad actually. So here is the crazily simple, yet decently tasty recipe</p>
<h3><em>Quick Almond Pie</em></h3>
<p><a href="http://blogbloggityblog.files.wordpress.com/2012/06/img_4633.jpg"><img class=" wp-image     alignright" title="Almond Pie" src="/assets/2012/06/img_4633.jpg?w=1014" alt="Image" width="300" height="200" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Preparation Time</strong>: 5 minutes</p>
<p><strong>Serves</strong>: 2 people</p>
<p><strong>Ingredients</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>8 Marie Biscuits</li>
<li>4 tsp Sugar</li>
<li>15 Almonds</li>
<li>2 tbsp Butter</li>
<li>4 tsp Milk (Replace with water/soy milk, if vegan)</li>
</ul>
<p><a href="http://blogbloggityblog.files.wordpress.com/2012/06/img_4640.jpg"><img class=" wp-image  alignleft" title="Almond Pie Slices" src="/assets/2012/06/img_4640.jpg?w=1014" alt="Almond Pie Slices" width="300" height="200" /></a></p>
<div><strong>Instructions</strong></div>
<ol>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Put the biscuits and the sugar in a mixer jar, and grind them to a fine powder</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Melt the butter and mix it together with the powder. Knead it lightly to get uniform consistencyÂ </span></li>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Break the almonds into small, but not too small pieces, and mix into the mixture</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Take a 4-inch microwavable plate, and press it at the bottom, spreading evenly</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Sprinkle the milk on the top evenly</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Microwave for 1.5 minutes</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Remove and cut into triangular pieces</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height:13px;">Serve hot, with vanilla ice cream</span></li>
</ol>
<div><span style="line-height:13px;"><strong>End Note</strong></span></div>
<p>You can probably replace almonds with walnuts or pistachios, to get the recipe for the corresponding pie</p>
