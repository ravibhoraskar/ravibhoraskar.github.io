<p>Benevolent?<br />
Or selfish? Hungry<br />
(like Shellfish)?</p>
<p>The coins fall<br />
into the bowl<br />
And clink<br />
Clink Clink Clink</p>
<p><em>But if they didn't?</em></p>
<p>In the forest, no one<br />
hears the leaf fall<br />
But if no one heard<br />
would the leaf fall at all?</p>
